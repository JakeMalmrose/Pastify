About Us

Pastify is a completely homemade streaming service that piggybacks off the work of Google. By using our own database and an unofficial API, we are able to stream music from YTMusic, create completely custom local playlists saved to accounts locally with no connection to Google.

Please note this website is a project by a group of students at Neumont College of Computer Science and is still under development. Some features may not work or malfunction. To report a bug, please press here!
